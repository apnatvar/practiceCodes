About Me

I am a 21-year-old pursuing Bachelor's in Computer Engineering at Trinity College Dublin. In my spare time, I can be found hiking and I seldom enjoy photography, which is why I decided to use my photos for this website. I have enjoyed working with code since I was first introduced to Java in the 10th grade. My college coursework has allowed to work with C, C++, Python, SQL, HTML, CSS, Verilog, Arduino, Raspberry Pi PICO and so much more. Working with code was exciting enough but it was not until later that I decided to purse Data Analytics.

In the winter of 2020, I was introduced to Big Data. I had heard the term earlier but never paid much thought to it. Once I began to understand the power of data I was instantly hooked. Since then, I have been working towards upskilling in the areas of Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, and Machine Learning.

The more exciting projects from my secret vault are included here. Hopefully, you will find them exciting or even inspiring. This will document my journey from a final-year student to a successful Data Analyst/Scientist. Manifestation is key!
